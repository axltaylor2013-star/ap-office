# Roblox MMO Launch Strategy
*Roscape Runeblocks Marketing Plan*

## Game Overview
- **Genre:** Fantasy MMORPG (RuneScape-inspired)
- **Platform:** Roblox
- **Status:** Complete, ready for launch
- **Key Features:** 10 skills, 90+ items, 21 monsters, PvP wilderness, prestige system
- **Target Audience:** 13-25 year olds, MMO/RPG fans, RuneScape nostalgia players

## Launch Timeline

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (1-2 weeks)
**Goals:** Build awareness, create anticipation
**Activities:**
- Content creator outreach (Scout researching 10-15 targets)
- Social media teasers (Mule creating campaign)
- Discord community setup (Forge building infrastructure)
- Beta testing with friends/family
- Game page optimization (title, description, thumbnails)

**Success Metrics:**
- 50+ Discord members pre-launch
- 5+ content creator commitments
- Social media following established
- Game page optimized for discovery

### Phase 2: Launch Day (Day 0)
**Goals:** Maximum visibility and first-day player influx
**Activities:**
- Coordinated social media blitz
- Content creator simultaneous streaming
- Discord launch party/events
- Reddit community posts
- Friends/family initial player boost

**Target Metrics:**
- 100+ concurrent players peak
- 500+ total visits day 1
- 25+ Discord members added
- 4+ star rating maintained

### Phase 3: Post-Launch Growth (Weeks 1-4)
**Goals:** Retain players, build community, iterate
**Activities:**
- Daily content updates/events
- Community feedback integration
- Regular social media engagement
- Influencer follow-up campaigns
- Player-generated content showcasing

**Success Metrics:**
- 50+ daily active players
- 200+ Discord community members
- Positive user reviews and feedback
- Growing social media engagement
- Word-of-mouth player referrals

## Marketing Channels

### Primary Channels
1. **Roblox Platform**
   - Optimized game page (icon, title, description)
   - Regular updates to stay in "Recently Updated"
   - Player comment engagement
   - Feature in relevant categories

2. **Social Media**
   - **TikTok/Instagram Reels:** Gameplay highlights (15-30s clips)
   - **Twitter/X:** Dev updates, community engagement, player spotlights
   - **YouTube:** Longer gameplay videos, tutorials, dev logs
   - **Reddit:** r/roblox, r/MMORPG, relevant gaming subreddits

3. **Discord Community**
   - Central community hub
   - Real-time player communication
   - Events and contests
   - Developer interaction

### Secondary Channels
- Gaming forums and communities
- Roblox developer communities
- MMO-focused Discord servers
- Gaming subreddits
- Content creator collaborations

## Influencer Strategy

### Target Criteria
- **Size:** 1K-50K subscribers (more responsive)
- **Content:** Roblox gaming, MMO/RPG focus
- **Audience:** Overlaps with our target demographic
- **Activity:** Regular posting, engaged community
- **Values:** Family-friendly, positive content

### Outreach Approach
1. **Personalized Messages**
   - Research their content style
   - Mention specific videos we liked
   - Explain why our game fits their audience
   - No mass DMs or generic pitches

2. **Value Proposition**
   - Early access to completed game
   - Direct developer interaction
   - Community role and recognition
   - Collaborative content opportunities

3. **Follow-up Strategy**
   - Respectful persistence (1-2 follow-ups max)
   - Relationship building over time
   - Authentic engagement with their content

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Challenges
- **Low initial traction:** Aggressive outreach, friend/family network activation
- **Negative feedback:** Quick response, transparent communication, rapid fixes
- **Content creator rejection:** Large outreach list, persistent follow-up
- **Competition:** Focus on unique features, community over metrics
- **Technical issues:** Thorough testing, rapid bug fix deployment

### Contingency Plans
- Alternative marketing channels if primary ones fail
- Community feedback integration for rapid iteration
- Technical support channels for player issues
- Backup content creator relationships
- Flexible strategy adjustment based on data
