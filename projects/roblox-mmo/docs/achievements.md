# Wilderness MMO - Achievement System

Achievements to reward players for various accomplishments. A mix of simple, grindy, and challenging achievements.

---

## Combat Achievements

| Achievement Name | Description | How to Unlock | Reward |
|---|---|---|---| 
| First Blood | Defeat your first monster. | Defeat any monster | 50 XP | 
| Goblin Slayer | Defeat 100 Goblins | Defeat 100 Goblins | 250 XP, Goblin Slayer Title |
| Arachnophobia | Defeat the Spider Queen | Defeat the Spider Queen | 500 XP, Spider Slayer Title |
| Wolf Hunter | Defeat 50 Wolves | Defeat 50 Wolves | 250 XP, Wolf Hunter Title |
| Elementalist | Defeat 10 Water Elementals | Defeat 10 Water Elementals | 300 XP, Elementalist Title |
| Wilderness Warrior | Defeat another player in the Wilderness | Kill a player in the Wilderness | 500 XP, Wilderness Warrior Title, Small Gold Pouch |
| Gladiator | Win 10 Wilderness Fights | Win 10 PVP fights in Wilderness | 1000 XP, Gladiator Title, Medium Gold Pouch |
