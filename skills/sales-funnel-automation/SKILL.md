# Sales Funnel Automation Specialist

Complete automation of Jeremy's AI automation sales process from lead capture to contract signature.

## Funnel Architecture Overview

### Stage 1: Lead Capture & Initial Response
**Trigger:** New inquiry via website, email, or SMS
**Automation:**
- Instant acknowledgment within 5 minutes
- Lead qualification survey delivery
- Calendar booking link for consultation
- CRM entry with source attribution
- Internal alert to Jeremy for high-value prospects

### Stage 2: Pre-Consultation Nurture
**Trigger:** Consultation booked but not yet completed
**Automation:**
- Confirmation email with meeting details and prep questions
- Day-before reminder with Zoom link and agenda
- 2-hour before SMS reminder with "running late?" option
- No-show follow-up sequence for rescheduling
- Pre-meeting qualification form to maximize consultation value

### Stage 3: Post-Consultation Follow-Up
**Trigger:** Consultation completed
**Automation:**
- Thank you email with meeting summary within 2 hours
- Custom proposal delivery within 24 hours
- Proposal follow-up sequence (Day 1, 3, 7, 14)
- Objection handling resources based on consultation notes
- Alternative package suggestions if initial proposal declined

### Stage 4: Proposal to Contract
**Trigger:** Proposal sent but not yet signed
**Automation:**
- Proposal opened tracking and engagement scoring
- Personalized follow-up based on time spent reviewing
- FAQ responses for common objections
- Social proof delivery (testimonials, case studies)
- Limited-time incentives for faster decision-making

### Stage 5: Contract to Implementation
**Trigger:** Contract signed and deposit received
**Automation:**
- Welcome email with implementation timeline
- Project kickoff meeting scheduler
- Pre-work assignment with clear instructions
- Implementation milestone notifications
- Client onboarding checklist with progress tracking

## Email Sequence Automation

### Lead Nurture Sequence (Pre-Consultation)
**Email 1 (Immediate):** Confirmation + Preparation
- Meeting confirmation and calendar details
- "What to expect" during consultation
- Brief questionnaire about current challenges
- Link to case studies relevant to their industry

**Email 2 (Day before):** Reminder + Agenda  
- Meeting reminder with Zoom link
- Consultation agenda and expected outcomes
- "Come prepared with" checklist for maximum value
- Jeremy's background and credentials

**SMS 1 (2 hours before):** Final reminder
- "Looking forward to our call at [TIME]"
- "Running late? Text LATE and we'll adjust"
- Direct line: (859) 428-7481

### Post-Consultation Sequence
**Email 1 (2 hours after):** Thank you + Summary
- Recap of discussed pain points and solutions
- Next steps timeline
- "Questions before proposal?" invitation
- Link to scheduling if additional discussion needed

**Email 2 (Next day):** Custom Proposal Delivery
- Personalized proposal based on consultation
- Video walkthrough of recommended solution
- ROI calculator with their specific numbers
- Clear next steps to move forward

**Email 3 (Day 3):** Value reinforcement  
- Additional case study similar to their situation
- Testimonial from client in same industry
- "Any questions about the proposal?" check-in
- Reminder of time savings and ROI projections

**Email 4 (Day 7):** Urgency + Alternatives
- Limited implementation slots remaining
- Alternative package options if budget constrained
- "Is there something holding you back?" question
- Direct phone number for immediate discussion

### SMS Integration Points
- **Consultation reminders** - 1 day and 2 hours before
- **Proposal follow-up** - 3 days after delivery if no response
- **Contract signature** - Thank you and next steps
- **Implementation updates** - Milestone completion notifications
- **Emergency contact** - Issues requiring immediate attention

## CRM Integration Workflow

### Lead Scoring Algorithm
**Qualification Points:**
- Budget confirmed: +20 points
- Decision authority: +15 points  
- Specific timeline: +10 points
- Multiple pain points: +15 points
- Previous automation experience: +5 points
- Referral source: +10 points

**Engagement Points:**
- Email opens: +1 point each
- Link clicks: +3 points each
- Proposal downloads: +5 points
- Video views: +5 points
- Response to emails: +10 points

**Hot Lead Threshold:** 50+ points triggers immediate Jeremy notification

### Activity Automation
- **Email tracking** - Opens, clicks, forwards
- **Website behavior** - Page visits, time spent, downloads
- **Proposal engagement** - Sections viewed, time spent per page
- **Communication history** - All touchpoints automatically logged
- **Next action scheduling** - Automated task creation for Jeremy

### Pipeline Management
- **Stage progression** - Automatic advancement based on actions
- **Stale lead identification** - Alerts for prospects inactive >14 days
- **Win/loss tracking** - Reasons captured for optimization
- **Revenue forecasting** - Weighted pipeline value calculations
- **Performance analytics** - Conversion rates by source and stage

## Proposal Automation System

### Dynamic Proposal Generation
**Input Parameters:**
- Company size and industry
- Identified pain points and solutions
- Budget range and timeline
- Current processes and desired outcomes
- ROI projections based on their numbers

**Template Selection:**
- **Basic Package ($2,200-5,000)** - Single process automation
- **Professional Package ($5,000-15,000)** - Multiple system integration  
- **Enterprise Package ($15,000-45,000)** - Complete business transformation
- **Custom Solutions** - Unique requirements outside standard packages

### Delivery Automation
