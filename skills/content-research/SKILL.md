# Content Research

## Platforms to Check

1. **TikTok** — Trending sounds, hashtags, formats in creative/editing niche
2. **Instagram Reels** — Top-performing reels in photo/video editing space
3. **YouTube Shorts** — Trending tutorials, before/after edits, AI tool demos
4. **X/Twitter** — Conversations about creative tools, freelance tips, AI updates

## Focus Areas

- Photo editing techniques and tools
- Video editing workflows and tutorials
- AI creative tools (image gen, video gen, upscaling, voice)
- Freelance market trends and tips

## Output Format

Save to `mule-output/trends-YYYY-MM-DD.md`:

```markdown
# Content Research — YYYY-MM-DD

## Trend 1: [Trend Name]
- **Platform**: TikTok / IG / YouTube / X
- **Description**: What's happening and why it's trending
- **Relevance**: How this connects to Kermicle Media's content
- **Content Idea**: Specific video/post concept Kermicle could create
- **Reference**: URL or search term

## Trend 2: ...
```

## Daily Workflow

1. Check each platform for trending content in focus areas
2. Identify 5-10 relevant trends or viral formats
3. Write up each trend with the output format above
4. Prioritize trends with highest relevance to Kermicle Media
5. Save to `mule-output/trends-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
6. Flag any urgent/time-sensitive trends (sounds about to peak, trending challenges)

## What Makes a Good Trend Pick

- **Actionable** — Kermicle can realistically create content around it
- **Timely** — Still rising or at peak, not already dying
- **On-brand** — Fits the creative/editing/media niche
- **Engaging** — High comment/share potential, not just views
