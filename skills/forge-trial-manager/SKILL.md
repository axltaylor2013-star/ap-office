# Forge Trial Manager

## Purpose
Post-sale bridge value system providing immediate AI access while customers wait for full system implementation. Builds familiarity, reduces buyer's remorse, and creates excitement for the complete solution.

## Trial Framework

### Trial Timing
**Activation:** Immediately after sale completion
**Duration:** Until full system implementation (typically 7-14 days)
**Access:** Direct Telegram access to @forge32bot
**Positioning:** "Early access to your AI team member"

### Trial Goals
1. **Immediate Value Delivery** - Customer gets benefits on Day 1
2. **AI Familiarization** - Comfort with AI before full system
3. **Usage Pattern Analysis** - Learn what they actually need
4. **Excitement Building** - "This is just ONE agent!"
5. **Smooth Transition** - Ready for full system on install day

## Trial Success Scenarios

### Scenario A: Light Usage (1-3 interactions/day)
**Response:** Personal outreach to offer guidance
**Message:** "I noticed you haven't used Forge much yet. Here are 3 specific ways he could help your [business type] right now..."

### Scenario B: Heavy Usage (10+ interactions/day)  
**Response:** Excitement building for full system
**Message:** "Wow! You're really putting Forge to work. Imagine when you have [X] agents plus automated workflows running 24/7..."

### Scenario C: Technical Difficulties
**Response:** Immediate support and education
**Message:** "I see you had some issues with [task]. Let me show you the best way to work with AI for that..."

### Scenario D: Scope Creep Requests
**Response:** Gentle boundaries with upgrade preview
**Message:** "Great question! That's exactly what your full system will handle automatically. For now, here's a manual approach with Forge..."

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "Forge isn't understanding my requests"
**Solution:** Provide specific prompt templates for their industry
**Follow-up:** "The full system includes pre-trained workflows for exactly this"

### "I don't have time to learn this right now"
**Solution:** Schedule brief 15-minute orientation call
**Follow-up:** "That's why we handle all setup - you just use it"

### "This seems too simple/basic"
**Solution:** Show advanced capabilities preview
**Follow-up:** "Wait until you see what the complete system can do"

### "I'm worried about the full installation"
**Solution:** Trial success = implementation confidence
**Follow-up:** "You're already using AI successfully - we're just expanding it"
