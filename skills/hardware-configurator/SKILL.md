## Configuration Process

### 1. Client Needs Assessment

**Discovery Questions:**
- What's your primary business type and industry?
- How many people will use the system regularly?
- What processes do you want to automate first?
- What's your planned growth over next 2 years?
- Do you have specific software requirements?
- What's your comfortable investment range?

### 2. Technical Requirements Analysis

**Workload Assessment:**
- **Light:** Email, scheduling, basic automation → Basic Mac Mini
- **Moderate:** CRM, reporting, content creation → Mac Mini Pro  
- **Heavy:** Multi-dept workflows, complex integrations → M4 Max Studio
- **Enterprise:** Mission-critical, compliance → Professional/Enterprise
- **AI-Intensive:** Custom models, research → NVIDIA Lab

**Performance Needs:**
- **Memory Requirements:** Based on simultaneous processes
- **Processing Power:** CPU vs. GPU intensive tasks
- **Storage Needs:** Data volume and access patterns  
- **Connectivity:** Integration complexity and bandwidth
- **Scalability:** Growth projections and expansion plans

### 3. Budget Optimization

**Value Engineering:**
- Start with client's stated budget range
- Show how next tier up provides disproportionate value
- Demonstrate ROI calculations for upgrade investment
- Offer payment plans or phased implementation
- Position as business investment, not expense

**Upgrade Path Planning:**
- Design system with future expansion capability
- Show clear upgrade path as business grows
- Minimize future migration costs
- Protect current investment value
