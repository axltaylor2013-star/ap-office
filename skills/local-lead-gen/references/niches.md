# Niche-Specific Talking Points

## Restaurants & Cafés

**Pain Points**: Bad food photography, inconsistent social posting, no video content, outdated menus online
**Talking Points**:
- "Customers eat with their eyes first — 75% of diners check a restaurant's Instagram before visiting"
- Professional food photography increases online orders by 30%+
- Short-form video (reels/TikTok) of food prep drives massive engagement
- Seasonal menu content keeps their feed fresh

**Services to Pitch**: Food photography, menu design, social media content packages, short-form video

## Real Estate

**Pain Points**: Generic listing photos, no personal brand, boring virtual tours, no video walkthroughs
**Talking Points**:
- Listings with pro photos sell 32% faster
- Agent personal branding differentiates in a crowded market
- Video walkthroughs and drone footage are now expected by buyers
- Consistent social presence builds referral pipeline

**Services to Pitch**: Property photography/video, agent branding, social content, drone footage

## Fitness Studios & Gyms

**Pain Points**: No transformation content, poor class/session footage, weak social presence
**Talking Points**:
- Before/after content is the #1 driver for gym sign-ups
- Class highlight reels build community and attract new members
- Trainer spotlight content builds personal connections
- User-generated content campaigns are low-effort, high-ROI

**Services to Pitch**: Transformation photography, class/session video, trainer profiles, social strategy

## Boutiques & Retail

**Pain Points**: Product photos look amateur, no lifestyle content, inconsistent brand aesthetic
**Talking Points**:
- Product photography quality directly impacts online conversion rates
- Lifestyle/styled shoots tell a brand story that drives loyalty
- Consistent visual branding across platforms builds trust
- Seasonal lookbooks and launch content create buzz

**Services to Pitch**: Product photography, lifestyle shoots, brand identity, social content

## Event Planners

**Pain Points**: Relying on client phone photos for portfolio, no behind-the-scenes content, no video recaps
**Talking Points**:
- Professional event coverage IS your portfolio — it's how you book the next gig
- Highlight reels are shareable marketing that sells itself
- Behind-the-scenes content shows your process and professionalism
- Vendor collaboration content expands your network reach

**Services to Pitch**: Event photography/videography, highlight reels, portfolio content, BTS content

## Local Services (HVAC, Plumbing, Landscaping, Salons)

**Pain Points**: No visual presence, look unprofessional online, no reviews/testimonials content
**Talking Points**:
- Before/after project photos build instant trust
- Video testimonials convert 3x better than text reviews
- A professional-looking Google Business profile wins more calls
- "Day in the life" content humanizes the brand

**Services to Pitch**: Project photography, testimonial videos, Google Business optimization, brand refresh
