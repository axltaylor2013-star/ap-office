# Game Community Builder

Build engaged communities around gaming projects for long-term success and retention.

## What This Skill Does
- Create Discord servers and community hubs
- Establish content calendars and engagement strategies
- Build relationships with players and content creators
- Organize events, contests, and community activities
- Turn players into advocates and word-of-mouth marketers

## When To Use
- Game approaching launch or newly launched
- Need to increase player retention
- Want to build loyal fanbase  
- Planning community events or updates
- Looking to get user feedback and ideas

## Community Platforms

### Discord Server Setup
**Essential Channels:**
- #announcements (read-only dev updates)
- #general (main chat)
- #gameplay-help (tips, questions)
- #screenshots (player achievements)
- #suggestions (community feedback)
- #bug-reports (structured feedback)

**Advanced Channels:**
- #trading (if game has economy)
- #guilds-recruitment (player groups)
- #events (contests, tournaments)
- #content-creators (YouTubers, streamers)

### Reddit Community
- Create r/[GameName] subreddit
- Post regular updates and highlights
- Answer player questions quickly
- Share development insights
- Cross-post to larger gaming subreddits

### In-Game Community Features
- Guild/clan systems
- Player leaderboards  
- Community challenges
- Player-created content showcases
- Social features (friends, messaging)

## Engagement Strategies

### Content Calendar
**Daily:**
- Monitor community channels
- Respond to questions/feedback
- Share player achievements

**Weekly:**
- Developer update post
- Community highlight reel
- Screenshot contest or challenge

**Monthly:**
- Major update announcement
- Community feedback survey
- Special events or tournaments

### Community Events
1. **Launch Week Special**: Double XP, exclusive items
2. **Screenshot Contests**: Best base, funniest moment, epic battle
3. **Community Challenges**: First to reach level X, team objectives
4. **Developer Q&A Sessions**: Live streams answering questions
5. **Player Spotlights**: Feature community members

### Influencer Outreach
**Target Creators:**
- Small Roblox YouTubers (1K-10K subs) more likely to respond
- TikTok gaming creators
- Gaming streamers on Twitch
- Roblox-focused content creators

**Outreach Strategy:**
- Personalized messages (not mass DMs)
- Offer exclusive early access
- Provide talking points about unique features
- No pressure - genuine relationship building
- Follow up appropriately

### Feedback Response Framework
**For Positive Feedback:**
- Thank them personally
- Share with development team
- Ask for specific examples
- Invite them to share more

**For Constructive Criticism:**
- Acknowledge the feedback
- Explain if/how you'll address it  
- Ask for clarification if needed
- Thank them for caring enough to help

**For Bug Reports:**
- Confirm you've seen it
- Ask for reproduction steps
- Provide timeline for fix if possible
- Follow up when resolved

## Growth Tactics

### Word-of-Mouth Amplification
- **Referral System**: In-game rewards for bringing friends
- **Social Sharing**: Easy screenshot/clip sharing tools
- **Achievement Broadcasting**: Auto-share major milestones
- **Community Challenges**: Team-based objectives

### User-Generated Content
- Encourage fan art and creations
- Share community content on official channels
- Create contests around user content
- Feature player builds/achievements

### Cross-Pollination
- Partner with complementary games
- Guest appearances in other communities
- Share audiences with similar developers
- Collaborate on events or challenges

## Metrics & KPIs
- Discord members and activity levels
- Reddit subscribers and engagement
- In-game social feature usage
- Community-generated content volume
- Player retention rates
- User acquisition from community sources

## Red Flags to Avoid
- Over-moderation (killing organic conversation)
- Ignoring negative feedback
- Only posting promotional content
- Not establishing clear community guidelines
- Abandoning channels once created
- Being defensive about criticism

Build genuine relationships, not just marketing funnels. A strong community will sustain your game long-term.
