**Runtime:** ~22 minutes at standard animation pacing

**Tone check:** Comedy-first with genuine heart in Act 6. The Jarvis-Jeremy dynamic should feel like an old married couple â€” bickering but inseparable.

**Key animation moments to nail:**

- The chat comments on Jeremy's livestream (more easter eggs for pause-happy viewers)

- Forge: Silent (communicates via on-screen text only)
- The Dog: No voice. Just chaos energy and impeccable comedic timing.
